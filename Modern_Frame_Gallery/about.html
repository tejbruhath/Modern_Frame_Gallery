About The Modern Frame

Celebrating contemporary art since 1995

Our History

The Modern Frame was established in 1995 by visionary art collector Isabella Martinez with a simple yet profound mission: to showcase contemporary art that challenges, inspires, and transforms.

What began as a small gallery in downtown has grown into one of the city's most respected contemporary art spaces. Our commitment to emerging artists and innovative exhibitions has made us a cultural cornerstone in the art community.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see." - Edgar Degas

Over the past three decades, we've hosted over 200 exhibitions featuring works from both established masters and emerging talents. Our gallery has become a platform for artistic expression and cultural dialogue.

Our Mission

The Modern Frame is dedicated to fostering artistic innovation and cultural dialogue. We believe that contemporary art has the power to reflect our times, challenge our perspectives, and inspire meaningful conversations about the world we live in.

Through our carefully curated exhibitions, educational programs, and community outreach, we strive to make contemporary art accessible to everyone while supporting the next generation of artistic talent.
